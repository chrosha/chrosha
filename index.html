Authentic African Chop. Bold Flavors.

Welcome to Lagos Grill & Lounge, previously known as Olarry Asun Spot. We've refreshed our look, but our commitment to serving Authentic African Chop remains stronger than ever.

We specialize in premium grilled meats, notably our famous Asun, bringing the vibrant, spicy, and unmistakable taste of Lagos straight to Joliet. All our meats are 100% Zabiha Halal. We also offer professional catering services for your special events. Whether you're craving a taste of home or looking to explore bold new flavors, we serve every dish with warmth and community spirit.

Come for the food, stay for the experience.
